StudyPal Mobile App

StudyPal is a student companion app tool designed to help students stay organized, manage their time better, and improve their study habits. It includes helpful features like a study timer, flashcards, a planner, a task list, and file compiler that organizes all the files related to a subject in one place. This makes it easier for students to access their study materials without wasting time searching through different folders.

Languages Used: • Java JavaScript, • SQLite
